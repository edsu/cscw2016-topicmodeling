Visiting museums as part of a group poses the challenge of managing engagement with exhibits while preserving group cohesion. We respond to this by reconfiguring the social dynamic of visiting with an experience designed specifically for groups, that invites the group members themselves to design and „gift‟ interpretations to one another. We present a trial of this experience with groups of family and friends at a museum.
